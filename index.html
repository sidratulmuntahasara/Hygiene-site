At our Hygiene Detergents Factory L.L.C established in Ajman, UAE, we share the belief that Quality Exceeds Value. Our company recognizes that promises made must be kept, and we are committed to exceeding our customers' expectations by delivering products of the highest quality.
Our mission is to build a company that will create opportunities and benefits for future generations, and we are dedicated to being an active member of our community. As a trusted provider of household cleaning products, we take pride in offering affordable and high-quality products along with excellent customer service to consumers, retail outlets, and other agencies.

  • We believe in using eco-friendly ingredients that are safe for both the environment and our customers.
  • We believe in continuous innovation and improvement to provide the most effective cleaning solutions.
  • We believe in fostering long-term relationships with our customers and partners based on trust, transparency, and mutual respect.
